Marine Insurance Solution

Role Experience Designer
Type Blockchain, Fintech
Focus UX Strategy, Prototyping

Overview

Digital product design for a blockchain-based marine insurance solution. I helped in idea conceptualization and digital prototype creation. We worked along with the clients to do research and facilitate design workshops to create the user experience of the platform. We identified personas and their journeys to design a user-focused solution.

The Business Problem

Marine insurance is a vast domain that includes many stakeholders like MNCs, brokers, insurers, re-insurers, and retrocessionals. The transfer of risk between these stakeholders is a complicated workflow.

The client wanted a simplified solution utilizing blockchain to offer maximum security for contracts, programs, declarations, and claims. The goal was to make it easier for participants to manage and exchange these critical documents.

Challenges & Expectations

Challenge: Understanding the complex marine insurance process and the role of each participant (broker, insurer, reinsurer, etc.) was critical. Getting a bird's-eye view of the entire process including all varied workflows was difficult. Taking a new approach to "program creation" was challenging but became a vital part of the project.

Expectation:

  • Develop a best-in-class strategy to handle the complicated ecosystem.
  • Provide an optimal way to communicate coverage requirements between brokers and insurers.
  • Deliver a functional, clickable prototype.

Process Followed

We followed a user-centered design approach, starting with research and persona definition, moving to information architecture, and then to detailed wireframing.
