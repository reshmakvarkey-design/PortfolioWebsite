Travel with Jane

Role Experience Designer
Type Mobile App, Travel

Overview

Jane is a free travel app that allows trip planners to book tickets for transport, attractions and tours via secure payment using credit cards. It allows users to subscribe for pocket wifi for their travel.

Business Problem

  • Provide a super app experience which offers various services under one umbrella
  • Provide the best way to get portable unlimited internet while abroad
  • Offer data connection in 130+ countries backed by leading network providers
  • Give an all-in-one experience where users can book hotel stays, transport, attractions, etc.
  • Provide amazing deals and user reviews during search
  • Promote the use of pocket Wi-Fi from different companies through the application

Challenge

  • How to provide a unique experience when there are so many travel-based applications available
  • Promoting pocket Wi-Fi along with other promising services
  • The biggest challenge of super apps is that they don't focus on one thing but rather many services

Expectations

  • Come up with a website design which showcases the features and benefits of the Jane app
  • Along with other services, promote the pocket Wi-Fi
  • Create a clickable prototype of an application and a one-page website

Key Application Features

  • Internet for travelers: Portable unlimited internet while abroad with global, secure, and unlimited data
  • 130+ destinations: Connect in multiple countries backed by leading networks
  • Convenience at your fingertips: Ship directly to your home or pick up from your favorite location
  • More than just WiFi: All-in-one app for hotel stays, attractions, transport, tours, travel insurance, accessories, and more
  • Digital Wallet: Secure payment integration

Process Followed

The design process included persona development, information architecture, user story mapping, and iterative prototyping from low to high fidelity wireframes.
