The Challenge

Problem Statement

Understanding the core challenges we needed to address to create a successful travel super app experience.

Market Saturation

How to provide a unique experience when there are so many travel-based applications available in the market.

Service Integration

Promoting pocket Wi-Fi along with other promising services without overwhelming the user experience.

Super App Complexity

The biggest challenge of super apps is that they don't focus on one thing but rather many services, which can confuse users.

Business Problem

  • Provide a super app experience offering various services under one umbrella
  • Offer the best portable unlimited internet while traveling abroad
  • Data connection in 130+ countries with leading network providers
  • All-in-one experience: hotels, transport, attractions, and more
  • Amazing deals with authentic user reviews during search
  • Promote pocket Wi-Fi from various partner companies
